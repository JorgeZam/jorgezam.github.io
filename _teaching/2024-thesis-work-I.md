A graduate course designed to guide Master's students through the early stages 
of their thesis work. Topics include the formulation of research objectives and 
hypotheses, development of the theoretical framework, literature review, and 
definition of expected results. Progress is monitored through structured 
evaluations at each stage. Taught in 2024 and 2025.
