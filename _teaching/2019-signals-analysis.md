An undergraduate course on discrete and continuous-time signal analysis. 
Topics include time-domain and frequency-domain analysis, digital and analog 
filter design, digital and analog modulations, and spatial signal analysis 
applied to images. Taught continuously from 2019 to 2025.

## Technologies and Tools
- **Languages:** MATLAB, Python
